Projects

Metropolis | 2023 |

Scoring Metropolis was a great opportunity to allow a couple of my favourite influences to inspire me, notably The BBC Radiophonic Workshop, Benn Burtt and Pierre Henry.

Particular aspects of their work I was interested in recreating included tape-based manipulation methods of sound generation, alongside utillising modern wavetable synthesis to design sounds that would fit seamlessly with the vintage experimental sci-fi aesthetic.

| Performance at The School Of Digital Art, Manchester | 2023 | A collaboration between me and several over musicians, pulling together our influences such as Krautrock, Electronic and Jazz influences to create an original piece of music.
| Lurpak Advert | 2022 | Creating kitchenware and food sounds to match the advert was a really fun excercise as it allowed me to interpret how certain scenes would sound with objects that don't make sounds that fit the clip.
