Here Comes Science! | 2025 |

With this project, I aimed to recreate the subconcious aesthetic that early 70s educational programs utilised.

This aesthetic incorporates heavily synthesised modular synthesis to create sound design to abstract visual, mostly microscopic biological lifeforms.

With 'Here Comes Science!' I assembled a compilation of clips and created the sound design in this style, however, my interpretation had the modern twist of exploring whilst incorporating modern sound design modules into this distinctive aesthetic.

Demonstrated confident technical skills with an extensive investigation of modular synthesis

Chris Gladwin, Experimental Sound Artist
