Arron Ware is an experimental Sound Designer and Foley Artist based in Manchester.

He draws influence from a broad range of artists and sound designers such as Ben Burtt, Lois & Bebe Barron and Andy Partridge, in order to incorporate elements of dissonance into his work whilst still engaging with viewers of all backgrounds.

Arron meticulously pieces together sounds to create a tactile sonic world that immerses the listener. His musical work ranges from brutalist, dissonant compositions to an almost minimalistic classical, Sophisti-Pop-esque style.
