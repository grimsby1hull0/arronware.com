




------------------------------- Give an example of a time you worked well as part of a team to achieve a shared goal -----------------------------

At Vinco during one event that was based in Ireland, I was working as a replay operator and we were having encoder connection troubles throughout the broadcast which lasted 4 hours, we quickly established what we would do if the visual signal would drop out from both cameras: I would switch to playing the highlights reel I had assembled with a technical difficulties graphic or we would show a logo background and display the statistics graphic which was being actively being updated by the external organisation running the program on top of this. The issues were mainly with one encoder and the second camera worked most of the time so we managed to produce something that was presentable and still conveyed the racing to the viewer.

Working under this environment where the engineers were frantically working to resolve the connection troubles was distracting and relatively stressful, I believe it developed our team communication and ability to work under stress.
